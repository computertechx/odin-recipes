Nutella air fryer brownies

Description:

sweet and savory deliciousness, 8 servings recipe by taste.com

Ingredients:

  • 150g (1 cup) plain flour
  • 225g (1 cup) white sugar
  • 3 eggs, lightly whisked
  • 300g (1 cup) Nutella
  • Cocoa powder, to dust (optional)

Steps:

  1. Lightly grease a 20cm round cake pan. Line the base with baking paper.
  2. Use a balloon whisk to whisk together the flour and sugar in a bowl. Make a well in the centre. Add the egg and Nutella. Use a large metal spoon to stir until combined. Transfer to the prepared pan and smooth the top.
  3. Preheat the airfryer to 160C. Bake the brownie for 40 minutes or until a skewer inserted in the centre comes out with a few crumbs sticking. Set aside to cool completely.
  4. Dust with cocoa powder, if using, and cut into pieces to serve.
  5. enjoy
